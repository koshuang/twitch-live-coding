### `core-plugin-api`

A `Plugin` includes:

- id: string;
- apis?: Iterable<AnyApiFactory>;
- register?(hooks: PluginHooks): void;
- routes?: Routes;
- externalRoutes?: ExternalRoutes;

主要是定義 `Routes` & `Api`
